Rectal and Vaginal drug delivery

A practical and comprehensive overview of our range of excipients for suppositories and pessaries.

Application

Lipid excipients for suppository and pessary formulations include hard fat bases, as well as excipients for alternative dosage forms for local application such as creams, gels, and tablets, to achieve a local or systemic effect.

Main functionalities at a glance

Process and formulation

Our bases and formulation expertise, used with suppository and pessary molding process, enable to:

  • simplify existing formulations by reducing the number of excipients
  • optimize the manufacturing process and reduce quality issues
  • obtain excellent product quality attributes including drug dissolution, i.e. efficacy
